design: art or engineering?
    design is considered to have two sides: form and function
    art is evaluated for originality and expressiveness, it exists for its own sake
    design, however, is valued for its fitness to a particular task

    design principles
        - learnability
            help people access, learn and remember the system
        - reduce user's cognitive load
            - visibility
            - consistency
            - affordance
            - familiarity
