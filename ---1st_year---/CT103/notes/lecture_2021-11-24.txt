math.h functions
    pow()   - raise a number to the power of another
    sqrt()  - square root
    sin()   - sine of an angle
    cos()   - cosine of an angle    // all in radians
    tan()   - tangent of an angle
    exp()   - returns the exponent on a value
    log()   - returns the natural log of a number

function-like macros
    declared similarly to object-like macros
    syntax
        #define <name> (<arguments>) (<function_itself>)
